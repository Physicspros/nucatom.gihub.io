Question 2 A car of mass 500 kg is moving with the speed of 30 kilometre per hour. Calculate its kinetic energy.

Question 3 A man of mass 70 kg runs up 45 steps each 15 centimetre of height. calculate is potential energy at the top 45 stairs.

Question 4. A Truck of mass 1500 kg is moving with the speed of 20 m/s. Calculate its kinetic energy .

Question 5 George can finish 2000 J and William can finish 5000 J of work in 30 minutes and 20 minutes respectively. calculate their power.

Question 6 An electric motor raises a mass of 500 kg to a height of 5 meter in 20 seconds. Calculate the power of motor.

Question 7 A Mass 500 gram is released from height of 7 meter. Calculate its velocity when it hits the ground.

Question 8 A rock of 55 ton start sliding from a height 200 meter on a mountain. On the way it loses it's 60% of energy due to friction. Calculate its velocity when it reaches at the base of mountain.

Question 9 A man takes 500 joules of energy to do 300 Joule of work. Calculate is efficiency.
